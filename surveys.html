Why Election Surveys Matter

Election surveys help political parties, candidates, media organisations and research institutions understand voter opinion before voting begins. Scientific sampling, structured questionnaires, experienced investigators and careful data analysis help provide useful insights for campaign planning. A good survey identifies important public issues, leadership preferences and voting behaviour.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pre poll survey?

A pre poll survey measures voter opinion before polling starts.

What is an opinion poll?

An opinion poll studies public opinion on candidates, parties and important issues.

What is an exit poll?

An exit poll interviews voters after they vote to estimate likely election results.

How are election surveys conducted?

They use scientific sampling, trained investigators, structured questionnaires and statistical analysis.

Can small political parties use survey services?

Yes. Survey findings help parties understand voters and improve campaign planning.
